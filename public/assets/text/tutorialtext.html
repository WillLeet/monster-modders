Movement Keys

- Left ----> 'A'
- Right ----> 'D'
- Jump ----> 'W'

Creating and Removing Platforms

To place a platform you can either:

1. 'Click and drag' the 'Add Platform' button to move a platform to your desired location
2. Click the 'Add Platform' button which will have a platform appear and then 'click and drag' the platform to move it to your desired location

To delete a platform:

Click the 'Remove Platform' button and then click the platform you would like to remove

Sandbox

This mode is for testing out the game features. In this single-player mode, you can add and remove platforms as you'd like as well as move around to test out the game. You may also find it adventageous to devise a plan on where to place your platforms to best find your way to the finish in mulitplayer mode!

Multiplayer

The best part of this game! In here, you will want to first select a room for you and other player's to join. A room can have anywhere between 2-4 players to get the game started. Once all players are ready, the game will commence when someone presses the button in the top left of the screen. At this point the 'Build Phase' starts. You will have 15 seconds to place and remove platforms at will to find a path to the finish. Once that timer concludes, it is a mad dash to the finish! The player who reaches the finish first is crowned the winner. If the timer runs out before anyone reaches the finish, the game will conclude with no winner :(
